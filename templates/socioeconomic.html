Terms & Conditions

1. Dogs and cats have health needs such as proper nutrition, medical care, grooming, and exercise. They also have emotional needs, including spending time with them, training them, and integrating them into your family life.

2. By adopting, you take responsibility for their needs. Ensure that you are willing and have the time and resources to meet these obligations.

3. Choose your next companion based on their personality and yours, as well as your personal needs and lifestyle. Do not base your decision solely on their physical appearance; the goal is to create harmony so that you and your family are happy.

4. Consider where or with whom you will leave your pet when you go on vacation and cannot take them with you.

5. Think about what would happen if you move to another city or a residence where pets are not allowed. Shelter dogs and cats deserve a second chance at life, and we want to ensure they find a responsible owner committed to caring for them for life.

If any of the above points raise doubts or concerns, it might not be the right time for you to take on this commitment. In the meantime, you can support this cause by volunteering at a shelter or virtually adopting by sponsoring the rehabilitation of an animal in need.
