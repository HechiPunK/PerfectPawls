About us

About us

Perfect Pawls, founded by four software developers as a school project, was created with the purpose of reducing the issue of stray animals, especially dogs and cats, by using technology to connect people looking to adopt pets with those seeking to give them a home. This project evolved into a platform dedicated to animal welfare, promoting responsible adoption and reducing abandonment in Chihuahua, Mexico. Our mission is to facilitate responsible pet adoption through a personalized matching system, enhancing the well-being of both animals and their owners. We aspire to be the leading adoption platform in the region, promoting adoption practices that significantly reduce the number of animals living on the streets. Our core values include responsibility, compassion, integrity, innovation, and community, elements that guide every action in our mission to raise awareness and encourage responsible pet ownership.
